Short and smart listing creation

A lot of page loads can really slow down the process, so we put everything in one place. One click to post to Craigslist and share

The Dashboard: where the magic happens

Keeping track of all the details and emails for one listing is hard, and gets exponentially more difficult if you have more than one. We put it all in one place so you can keep track of each and every listing easily and know what's going on. You can edit, re-post, delete, and mark an item as sold as well as sort your listings by date, category, title, and responses.

Respond to emails

No more sifting through all your emails trying to find the ones related to each of your listing and no more headaches trying to remember who emailed what: it's all here. You can view all your messages related to each listing, keep track of how-row much each buyer offered, and respond right in the dashboard. When it's sold, you can even email everyone else at once to let them know.
