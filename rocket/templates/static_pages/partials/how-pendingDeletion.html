Better Listing Creation

Everything on one page, including the preview

A lot of page loads can really slow down the process, so we put everything in one place. You can even toggle to preview mode in the same window so you can see what your listing will look like as you make changes.

Smart price suggestions

Knowing how much to sell your item for can be the difference between getting it sold today and having it sitting in your garage forever. We pull data from sites like Craigslist, Ebay, and Amazon to find the going rate for your item.

Category-based specifications

We give you prefilled, optional specs based on the category so you know what info buyers need to know about your item.

More control of photos

Photos are a huge part of your listing, so we want to make sure you have full control. Easily upload multiple, high-res photos, so you if you need to make edits you don't need image editing software .

Automagically post to Craigslist

Get that awesome listing out there! It's just one click to post to Craigslist and share on Twitter and Facebook.

Better Listing Management

Keep track of everything in the dashboard

Keeping track of all the details and emails for one listing is hard, and gets even worse as you make mulitiple listings. We put it all in one place so you can keep track of every listing. You can edit, re-post, delete, and mark an item as sold as well as sort your listings by date, category, title, and responses.

Respond to emails

No more sifting through all your emails trying to find the ones related to each of your listing and no more headaches trying to remember who emailed what: it's all here.

All New Seller Profiles

Seller profiles for more trust

Everyone feels more comfortable when they know who's on the other end, especially when you're meeting up to show-row and sell an item. We made optional seller profiles so that you can let buyers know you're a real person and worth buying from.
