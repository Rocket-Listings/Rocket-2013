Tips for a great listing

  • Good photos are the most important part of any great listing. Make sure your pictures are:
    • High resolution
    • Well lit
    • feature scratches and defects
    • feature labels of product info and accessories
  • The devil is in the details. Every fact you can list off about your product will usually result in a better sell.
  • Try and include why you're selling the item and any other interesting facts.
