One page to create a listing

Tired of seemingly endless pages to click through when you're creating a listing? So are we. That's why we put image upload, category selection, the listing creation form, a listing preview, and publish button on one page.

One click to post to Craigslist

Once you've created a listing, you don't need to leave our site to list it anywhere else. We currently have Craigslist autoposting and Facebook and Twitter sharing, with more options like Ebay and Etsy on the way.

One dashboard to manage the sale

The hardest part of actually selling something is dealing with the deluge of emails after you list it. We made a dashboard where you can:

  • Manage, edit, and update all your listings
  • Keep track of all the potential buyers interested in each listing
  • Reply to emails through the dashboard so they don't get lost in your inbox.
